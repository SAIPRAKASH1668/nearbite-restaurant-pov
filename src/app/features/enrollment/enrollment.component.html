Terms & Conditions

The following terms will be included in the downloaded document.

1. About YumDude

YumDude is a food delivery platform built exclusively for Nandyal. We connect local restaurants with customers and handle delivery. By enrolling, you agree to these terms.

2. Commission & Pricing

The commission agreed at enrollment is all-inclusive and covers:

  • 18% GST on the platform service
  • 2% payment gateway / transaction charges

No extra charges will be levied. The restaurant must not charge customers more than the prices listed on YumDude.

3. Settlement of Payments

YumDude will settle payments as follows:

  • First 2 weeks after enrollment: Daily settlements (next business day)
  • After the first 2 weeks: Every Monday, for all orders completed the previous week (Monday to Sunday)

Settlements are made via bank transfer or UPI to the details provided at enrollment. Discrepancies must be reported within 3 days of receiving the settlement statement.

4. What the Restaurant is Responsible For

The restaurant is solely responsible for:

  • Missing items — any ordered item not present in unzipped package (zip tie/cello tape seal is mandatory)
  • Incorrect items — wrong dish, variant, or quantity
  • Food quality — stale, undercooked, overcooked, or spoiled food
  • Food safety & hygiene — maintaining a valid FSSAI license and clean kitchen standards
  • Packaging — ensuring food is packed well to minimise spillage risk
  • Preparation time — keeping prep time within reasonable limits and updating in the app

If a customer complaint is found to be the restaurant’s fault, the refund cost will be adjusted from the next settlement.

5. What YumDude is Responsible For

YumDude takes responsibility for:

  • Food spillage during delivery caused by our delivery partner’s mishandling
  • Significant delivery delays caused by our delivery network (not by late food preparation)
  • Orders not reaching the customer due to delivery partner error

In such cases, YumDude will handle the customer resolution at its own cost. YumDude is not responsible for food quality, taste, or packaging.

6. Menu & Availability

The restaurant must keep its menu on the platform accurate and updated at all times. Unavailable items must be marked out of stock immediately to avoid complaints. YumDude may temporarily hide restaurants that consistently fail to fulfil orders.

7. Order Acceptance

Once an order is accepted, the restaurant must fulfill it. Repeated post-acceptance cancellations may lead to suspension. If the restaurant is closed or unable to take orders, it must mark itself as closed on the app in advance.

8. Ratings & Quality

Customers can rate their experience. Restaurants with a consistent rating below 3.0 for more than 30 days may be reviewed. Continued low ratings may result in removal from the platform.

9. Offers & Coupons

YumDude may run offers, discounts, and promotional campaigns on any restaurant listed on the platform at its sole discretion, without prior notice to or approval from the Restaurant Partner. The cost of such app-initiated offers shall be borne entirely by YumDude and shall have no impact on the Restaurant Partner’s revenue or settlement amounts.

The Restaurant Partner may also request YumDude to run restaurant-specific offers or promotions by reaching out to the YumDude team. The cost of such restaurant-initiated offers shall be borne entirely by the Restaurant Partner and will be adjusted from the Restaurant Partner’s settlement.

10. Termination of Partnership

Either party may end this partnership with 14 days’ written notice. YumDude may immediately suspend a restaurant without notice if:

  • There is a serious food safety violation
  • The FSSAI license has expired or been revoked
  • There is fraud, order manipulation, or misuse of the platform

11. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep the agreed commission rate and any shared business data confidential and not disclose it to third parties.

12. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of India. Any disputes will first be resolved amicably. If unresolved, jurisdiction shall be Nandyal, Andhra Pradesh.

13. Changes to These Terms

YumDude may update these terms from time to time with at least 7 days’ advance notice to restaurant partners. Continued use of the platform implies acceptance of the updated terms.
