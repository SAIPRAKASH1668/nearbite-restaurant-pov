8. Payment Settlement Process

Order Value collected by YumDude shall be passed to the Restaurant Partner after deducting the Agreed Commission Percentage (all-inclusive, no additional GST or fees), Restaurant Coupon discounts, applicable taxes, and any other agreed amounts.

Weekly Settlement (Default)

Payments collected from Sunday 12:00 AM to Saturday 11:59 PM of a given week shall be processed the following Sunday and delivered to the Restaurant Partner by Sunday evening 6:00 PM. If the Payment Settlement Day falls on a bank holiday, the settlement shall be processed on the next working day.

Daily Settlement (By Mutual Agreement)
