How printing works

  • KOT (Kitchen Order Ticket) prints on the kitchen printer immediately when you accept an order.
  • Customer Bill prints a full tax invoice with item breakdown and totals.
  • Both documents are sent automatically — no manual action needed.
  • Supported: 80 mm & 58 mm ESC/POS Bluetooth thermal printers (Xprinter XP-58, XP-80, Epson TM-T20, TVS RP-3200, etc.)
