How printing works

  • KOT (Kitchen Order Ticket) prints on all KOT printers immediately when you accept an order.
  • Customer Bill prints on all Bill printers — a full tax invoice with item breakdown and totals.
  • Both documents are sent automatically — no manual action needed.
  • Supported: 80 mm & 58 mm ESC/POS printers via Bluetooth (Xprinter XP-58/80, Epson TM-T20, TVS RP-3200), USB cable (OTG adapter on phones), or Network/WiFi (Epson TM-T82X, TM-T88 — works from both the Android app and the browser via Epson ePOS SDK).
  • You can add the same printer to both pools to receive both KOT and Bill.
