YumDude Rider — Privacy Policy

App: YumDude Rider · Package: app.rork.honesteats.rider · Version: 1.0.4 · Effective: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how YumDude ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information from delivery riders who use the YumDude Rider Android / iOS application ("the App"). By using the App you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1 About the App

YumDude Rider is a dedicated delivery-partner application that enables registered riders to:

  • Receive, accept, and complete food-delivery orders in real time
  • Share their live GPS location to facilitate order matching and customer tracking
  • View earnings, settlement history, and delivery records
  • Manage their profile and KYC documents required for onboarding
  • Communicate with restaurants and customers via the platform

The App is intended solely for adult delivery partners (18 years and older) who have entered into a partner agreement with YumDude.

2 Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Identity: First name, last name, date of birth
  • Contact: Mobile phone number (primary identifier), email address, residential address
  • Government-issued ID (KYC): Aadhaar card number and photo, PAN card number and photo — required under Indian law for gig-worker verification
  • Payment details: UPI ID for earnings disbursement
  • Profile updates: Any edits made on the Edit Profile screen

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Precise GPS location (foreground & background): Latitude, longitude, speed, and heading — continuously while the rider is "Online"
  • Device token: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to deliver push notifications
  • Session data: Login state, rider ID, JWT authentication token stored on-device
  • Usage and delivery data: Orders accepted, rejected, or completed; timestamps; delivery routes (inferred from location stream)
  • Earnings data: Per-delivery payout, settled and unsettled amounts
  • App integrity: Firebase App Check attestation token (prevents backend API abuse)

2.3 Information From Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication: Phone-number OTP verification result
  • Firebase Messaging: Delivery of order-assignment push notifications
  • Google Maps (deep-linked): When a rider taps "Navigate", Google Maps opens; data shared with Google is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4 How We Use Your Information

  • Account & authentication: Phone OTP via Firebase Auth; session maintained with a JWT token
  • KYC & compliance: Aadhaar/PAN details verified as required under Indian law for platform workers
  • Order operations: Matching orders to the nearest online rider; relaying addresses; computing ETA
  • Live tracking: Sharing the rider's real-time location with the customer and restaurant during an active delivery only
  • Earnings & payments: Calculating delivery fees, maintaining ledger, initiating UPI settlements
  • Push notifications: Alerting riders to new assignments, order updates, and platform communications
  • Support & disputes: Reviewing delivery history and location logs when a dispute is raised
  • Service improvement: Aggregate, anonymised analytics to improve routing and matching algorithms

5 Location Data — Detailed Explanation

We collect your precise GPS location (including in the background) while you are signed in and have set your status to "Online". Location tracking stops as soon as you go "Offline" or log out. A persistent notification is always shown while the background service is running.
  • Data points sent: Latitude, longitude, speed (km/h), heading (degrees) — approximately every 5–30 seconds depending on movement
  • Transmission: HTTPS-encrypted PUT request to https://api.yumdude.com/api/v1/riders/{id}/location
  • Retention: Last known location stored for operational purposes. Historical logs retained for up to 90 days for dispute resolution, then automatically purged.
  • Customer visibility: Customers see your live position only while they have an active order assigned to you. This stops immediately when the order is completed or cancelled.
  • No advertising use: We do not sell location data to advertisers or data brokers.

7 Data Storage & Security

  • All network communication is encrypted in transit via HTTPS / TLS 1.2+
  • Authentication uses short-lived JWT bearer tokens; tokens are validated server-side on every request and invalidated on logout
  • Stale or reinstalled-app sessions are detected and invalidated automatically (re-login required)
  • KYC document images are transmitted over HTTPS and stored on access-controlled servers encrypted at rest
  • Firebase App Check prevents unauthorised clients from accessing backend APIs
  • On-device data (session tokens, rider ID) is stored in Android AsyncStorage — not in external or cloud-accessible storage

8 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only as follows:

  • With customers: Your first name and real-time location (during active delivery only)
  • With restaurants: Your first name and estimated arrival time
  • With payment processors: UPI ID for disbursement only
  • With Firebase / Google: Phone number (auth), FCM device token (notifications), App Check attestation
  • Legal obligation: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority under Indian law (including the DPDP Act 2023)

9 Data Retention

  • Active account data: Retained for as long as the rider's account is active
  • Location history: Up to 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Order & earnings history: Retained for 3 years for legal and financial compliance
  • KYC documents: Retained as required under applicable Indian labour and financial regulations
  • Account deletion: Contact us at the address in Section 12. We will action deletion requests within 30 days, subject to legal retention requirements.

10 Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate personal data via Edit Profile in the App
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke location permission at any time via Android System Settings → App Permissions; note this will disable location-dependent features
  • Grievance redressal: Raise concerns with our Data Protection Officer (see Section 12)

11 Children's Privacy

YumDude Rider is intended for adults (18 years and older) only. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately for account deletion.
